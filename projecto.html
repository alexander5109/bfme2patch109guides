Rules:

  1. 1. Whatever is written inside «A» doesn't stack with «A». «B» doesn't stack with «B». Therefore debuff doesnt stack with debuff, neutralizing a category is another deal and will be informed by the tooltip.
  2. 2. Specials of «BUFF» category: ingame they have unique names or no names at all, means they stack with anything else.
  3. 3. Specials with no category: nothing cancel them, stack with anything. If they do neutralize any modifier category, tooltip will mention it.
  4. 4. FreezignRain is just another Debuff, but ALSO neutralices BUFF ... either damage, armor or uniques(since BT2DC or 1.07)
  5. 5. Warg howl cancels allied «Damage LEADERSHIP» (since BT2DC or 1.07)
  6. 6. Elf wood cancels allied «Armor LEADERSHIP» (since v3.00)
  7. 7. All other Unique «LEADERSHIP» or Unique «BUFF» stack with anything.
  8. 8. Any other modifier...negative or positive that isn't on the table...means it stacks with anything and nothing cancel it.
